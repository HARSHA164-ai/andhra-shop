Kondapalli Toys

Kondapalli Toys are traditional handcrafted wooden toys from the Kondapalli village in Andhra Pradesh, India. Made from soft and lightweight Tella Poniki wood, these toys are renowned for their vibrant colors, intricate detailing, and cultural storytelling. For generations, skilled artisans of Kondapalli have been handcrafting toys that depict animals, mythological characters, village scenes, and festival figures. Each toy is carefully carved, painted, and polished, making it not just a plaything, but a piece of art and heritage.
