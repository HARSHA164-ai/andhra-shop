Budithi Brassware

Budithi Brassware is a traditional handicraft from Budithi village in Andhra Pradesh, India, known for its exquisite brass artistry and cultural heritage. Crafted by skilled artisans, Budithi Brassware combines precision, durability, and elegance, creating decorative and functional items that are cherished for generations. Each piece is handmade using traditional techniques, including pouring molten brass into molds, detailed engraving, and polishing, ensuring that every item is unique and timeless. Budithi artisans are known for producing a wide range of items, from utensils, lamps, and idols to intricate decorative pieces, all reflecting the rich artistic tradition of Andhra Pradesh.
